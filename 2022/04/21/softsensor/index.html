Soft Sensor Illustration

Description

I created a set of illustrations for a scientific article in the field of soft sensors and biomedical engineering in which I was also one of the main authors, in addition to being the designer.

Full title of the article: A novel fully 3D, microfluidic-oriented, gel-based, and low cost stretchable soft sensor
The article is free to download! Download the paper

The mentioned sensor is a soft sensor that has canals inside it that are filled with a special gel. This sensor is able to measure all of the stretch, twist, and pressure features.

The ultimate aim of this project was to present the novel proposed soft sensor in the best possible way so that the article and product itself could attract more attention and citation.

Fabrication Process

The depicted figure illustrates the fabrication procedure of the proposed soft sensor. In some steps the real image of the sensor in process is shown. Extra details of fabrications is mentioned in the caption of the figure
