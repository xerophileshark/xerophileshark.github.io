The Microfluidic article

Description

I created a set of illustrations for a scientific article in the field of soft sensors and biomedical engineering in which I was also one of the main authors, in addition to being the designer.

Full title of the article: Rapid prototyping of low-cost digital microfluidic devices using laser ablation
The article can be accessed via IEEE Xplore Download the paper

The goal of this project was to present the novel proposed prototyping method in the best possible way, so that the article and final chip itself could attract more attention and citation.
